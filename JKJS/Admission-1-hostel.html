1.At the time of admission,Parents must carry student's general details with father,mother or parents passport size photo.
2.At the time of admission,Parents must come with following items:-
(i)Bed-item
(ii)Box,Lock and key
(iii)Mug-Bucket
(iv)Plate-cup,Glass
(iv)Torch,Mosquito-Net
(v)Mirror-comb
3.No,any concession in hostel fee for any student if he is absent from hostel
4.During Vacation or taking leave from hostel parents presence is compulsory
5.Admission fee and one month hostel fee shall not be refundable at any condition.
6.Parents can talk with their child in between 6 PM to 10 PM on hostel Mobile number.
7.Personal item(soap,oil),Book,Copy medicine etc is not free. Parents have to pay for it.
