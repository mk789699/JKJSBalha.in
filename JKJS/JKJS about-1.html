John Keats Junior school is a co-Educational Junior School affiliated to government of Bihar for educating students from class 1st to 8th. The school has been operating officially under the yugeshwar singh memorial education and human welfare Trust. School also prepares students for competitive exams like Navodaya Vidyalaya class 6th entrance exam under direction of qualified trained teacher.
John Keats Junior school established on 6th January,1996 in response to the continuous demand of rural people of area.The school is situated beside State highway-77 in Balha-Madhsudanpur ,Pupari,Sitamarhi from VIA-Janakpur road.A team of highly qualified and trained faculties are determined to nourish curious minds. Students are also provided with high class hostel facilities for their all arround development.School aims to enhance physical,mental and spiritual growth of child through a perfect amalgamation of academics,sports,co-circular activities.

Affiliation

John keats Junior School got affiliation from Government of Bihar with registration no SIT/PUP/009 in 2014.
