John Keats Junior School is providing excellent educational facility and opportunities that are highly necessary for students.We aim to shape future of our students in such a manner that they can face and overcome upcoming challenges in life.

Our vision is based on these following values:-

1.Focus:- Students are being focused to reach their diverse goals in smart manner.
2.Excellence:-Maintain a high standard of integrity and performance leading to the achivement of academics.
3.Life long Learning:-Being as enthusiastic and independent thinkers that leads for their growth.
4.Diversity:-Diversity gives a challenge to students by fostering a competitive envirnonment among students for JNV preparation.
