About John Keats Junior School
John Keats Junior school is a co-Educational Junior School affiliated to government of Bihar for educating students from class 1st to 8th. The school has been operating officially under the yugeshwar singh memorial education and human welfare Trust. School also prepares students for competitive exams like Navodaya Vidyalaya class 6th entrance exam under direction of qualified trained teacher....

Affiliation

John keats Junior School got affiliation from Government of Bihar with registration no SIT/PUP/009 in 2014.
